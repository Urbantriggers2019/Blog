Wind Power

The wind power database that was launched in 2018 contains all the wind turbines currently in operation. Eight new wind turbines were commissioned during 2018, one of which was an extension of an existing plant. According to figures reported to NVE, an additional 8 wind turbines are expected to be commissioned during 2019, with a total installed capacity of 1,337 MW. This will result in an additional 4.5 TWh of wind power production, so that by the end of 2019 we expect a total production from Norwegian wind turbines of almost 10 TWh.
